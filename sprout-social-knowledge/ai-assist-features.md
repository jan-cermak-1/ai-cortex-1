## Overview

Sprout Social's AI Assist is a suite of **assistive AI features** embedded throughout the platform. Key distinction: these are **not agentic** — they always require human review and approval before action. "Human in the loop always."

All AI Assist features are powered by Sprout's integration with **OpenAI**.  
*Note: Account owner must enable OpenAI Integration to use AI features.*

---

## Publishing AI Features

### Generate Post by AI Assist
**Where:** Compose window  
**What it does:** Uses AI to generate post text content based on:
- Top performing posts from your account
- Custom topics you specify

**Output:** Multiple caption variants to choose from  
**Use cases:** Breaking through writer's block, maintaining consistent brand voice, onboarding new team members who may not know the brand voice

**Real result (Sprout internal):** 72 hours saved per quarter on content creation; $132,000 annual value from customer care improvements using AI-assisted content.

### Generate by AI Assist (Alt Text)
**Where:** Compose window, image upload  
**What it does:** Automatically generates alt text for images to improve accessibility  
**Options:** Review suggestion → use as-is or click "Regenerate" for alternative options  
**Why it matters:** Required for accessibility compliance; previously manual and time-consuming  
**Also used by:** Caesars Entertainment for brand and careers accounts at scale

### Enhance by AI Assist (Compose)
**Where:** Compose window on drafted text  
**What it does:** Improves existing post copy with 6 enhancement actions:
1. Rephrase
2. Shorten
3. Expand
4. Change tone (options vary by platform)
5. Correct grammar
6. Translate

**Use cases:** Adapting the same message for different platforms, improving readability before publishing

### Generate Subtitles by AI Assist
**Where:** Video upload in Compose  
**What it does:** One-click video subtitle generation via OpenAI integration  
**Why it matters:** Increases video accessibility and engagement (most social video is watched without sound)

### Suggested Hashtags
**Where:** Compose window (Instagram and TikTok)  
**What it does:** Analyzes last 30 days of your account's most frequently and recently used hashtags → populates suggestions in Compose  
**Limitation:** Desktop only  
**Use case:** Quick, data-backed hashtag selection without manual research

### Optimal Send Times
**Where:** Scheduling interface  
**What it does:** Analyzes your audience's engagement patterns → recommends best times to post for maximum reach  
**Output:** Multiple time slots ranked by predicted engagement  
**Used by:** SKP Creative (agency managing hundreds of client profiles), Caesars Entertainment

## Smart Inbox / Engagement AI Features

### Enhance by AI Assist (Smart Inbox)
**Where:** Reply compose area in Smart Inbox  
**What it does:** Generates reply options with different tone configurations:
- Draft initial response
- Rephrase existing draft
- Adjust tone (professional, empathetic, friendly, direct)
- Change length

**Use case:** Speeding up response time while maintaining quality; helping agents unfamiliar with social tone  
**Real result (Paychex):** Care agents not trained in social media could craft social-appropriate, brand-aligned responses  
**Quote:** "We're not trained on social media. Our agents are thinking about it with a service mindset. Using Sprout's AI functionality to help rewrite responses makes them more personable." — Michelle Latoy, 24/7 Enterprise Manager, Paychex

### Summarize by AI Assist
**Where:** Smart Inbox, case view  
**What it does:** Generates a 2-3 sentence summary of messages longer than 800 characters  
**Use case:** Care agents quickly understanding long customer complaint threads without reading every message; useful for case handoffs

### Sentiment in Smart Inbox
**Where:** Smart Inbox message list  
**What it does:** Classifies each incoming message as: Positive / Negative / Neutral / Unknown  
**Use cases:** 
- Prioritize negative messages for faster response
- Route by sentiment to specialized teams
- Filter inbox to focus on critical issues  
**Real result (Honda US):** Combined with intent analysis → reduced daily inbox management from 5h to 2h (40% time reduction)

## Social Listening AI Features

### Analyze by AI Assist (for Listening)
**Where:** Listening topic view  
**What it does:** Synthesizes topic data to surface:
- Most significant words and phrases
- Key hashtags and emojis
- Smart Categories (AI-identified content groups)
- Trend summaries

**Use case:** Instead of manually scanning thousands of mentions, get a structured summary of what's gaining traction and why  
**Real result (Benefit Cosmetics):** Surface significant words, phrases, hashtags, emojis and Smart Categories within listening topics — clearer summaries of what's gaining traction

### Recommend by AI Assist (Listening query builder)
**Where:** Listening query builder  
**What it does:** Generates keyword suggestions for building/expanding Listening queries using OpenAI  
**Use case:** Build better queries faster; discover relevant terms you might have missed  
**Also useful for:** Crisis management — quickly expand monitoring keywords during an emerging situation

### Spike Alerts in Listening
**Where:** Listening topic alerts  
**What it does:** Notifies you when there are significant changes in:
- Topic mention volume
- Potential impressions
- Engagement rate
- Sentiment score

**Why it matters:** Catch brand issues or opportunities before they escalate  
**Crisis use case:** Purdue University used Listening alerts to detect spikes and get ahead of potential issues

### Sentiment in Listening
**Where:** Listening topic analysis  
**What it does:** Classifies every listening message as Positive / Negative / Neutral / Unknown  
**Use case:** Quickly understand how people react to your content, campaigns, or brand

### Smart Categories in Listening
**Where:** Listening topic analysis  
**What it does:** Identifies, classifies, and groups entities in your Listening Topic  
**Output:** Shows which categories (products, people, places, topics) appear most often  
**Use case:** Finding recurring themes across thousands of unstructured social mentions

### AI Spam Detection in Listening
**Where:** Listening topics  
**What it does:** Automatically filters out spam, bot-generated content, and noise from Listening topics  
**Why it matters:** Improves signal-to-noise ratio; ensures listening data is based on real human conversations

### AI Industry Themes
**Where:** Listening module  
**What it does:** Surfaces trending topics within a specific industry  
**Use case:** Stay ahead of industry conversations; find content opportunities
