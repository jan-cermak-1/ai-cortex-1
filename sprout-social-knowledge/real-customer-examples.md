# Sprout Social: Real Customer Examples with Measurable Outcomes

> Source: https://sproutsocial.com/insights/examples-of-brands-using-sprout-social-ai/

## Overview

These 7 examples from real Sprout Social customers demonstrate how AI delivers measurable value in practice. Useful for benchmarking what Emplifi Cortex should achieve.

---

## 1. Honda US — Intent-Based Inbox Management

**Industry:** Automotive  
**Team size:** 4 people managing all U.S. social  
**Challenge:** Inbox triage absorbed hours each day as volume increased; team of 4 couldn't keep up

**Sprout AI features used:**
- AI-powered tagging and intelligent filters
- Intent analysis
- Hybrid automated rules (combining AI signals + rule-based routing)
- Sentiment analysis for urgency routing

**Results:**
- **Inbox management reduced: 5 hours/day → 2 hours/day** (40% reduction)
- **91% high-quality engagement action rate** — well above industry benchmark
- Tagging + filters surface high-value interactions (comments, shares, DMs) instantly
- Messages reach right team members via intent analysis + hybrid rules
- Freed capacity redirected to content planning and proactive analysis

**Quote:**
> "The fact that the Sprout team listened and really understood our process, which then helped influence enhancements to the tool, that's a game changer." — Allie Coulter, Enterprise Social Media Practice Lead, Honda US

**Key insight for Emplifi:** 40% time reduction in inbox management is achievable without full agentic AI — smart routing + AI triage is sufficient. The Cortex "Community Inbox Zero" flow should promise a similar outcome.

## 2. Caesars Entertainment — Content Creation + Listening at Scale

**Industry:** Hospitality / Entertainment  
**Challenge:** Multiple brands, accounts, campaigns; high volume of conversations; accessibility requirements; employer brand accounts alongside consumer accounts

**Sprout AI features used:**
- **Trellis AI Agent** for listening analysis (plain-language queries replacing manual dashboard work)
- **AI Assist** for campaign and employer brand copy generation (multiple variants per post)
- **Generate by AI Assist** for alt text generation on images (accessibility)

**Results:**
- Significantly reduced time spent on social listening analysis
- Teams can generate on-brand copy variations without repetition
- Employer brand content maintains consistency across career accounts
- Alt text creation scaled across entire content library

**Quote:**
> "Trellis has provided so much ease in being able to extract a lot of the information that would normally take me so much time and bandwidth." — Bianca Shaw, Head of Social Media & Digital Reputation, Caesars Entertainment

**Key insight for Emplifi:** Trellis is specifically valuable for listening-heavy teams managing multiple brands. The "Executive Weekly Briefing" and "Audience Insights Board" Cortex flows address this same need.

## 3. SKP Creative (Agency) — Publishing Optimization + Sentiment Analysis

**Industry:** Agency (full-service: social, digital, reputation management, crisis communications)  
**Challenge:** Managing hundreds of client profiles across multiple networks; enormous message volume; time-consuming reporting that spans organic, paid, and competitive data

**Sprout AI features used:**
- **Optimal Send Times** — data-backed scheduling decisions per client
- **Sentiment analysis** in Smart Inbox — quickly understand tone and urgency across incoming messages
- Premium Analytics — combining organic, paid, and competitive metrics in one view

**Results:**
- Data-backed publishing decisions for all clients without manual research
- Message volume managed efficiently across hundreds of profiles
- Clear priority signals for response teams

**Quote:**
> "When you're managing hundreds of client profiles across multiple networks, message volume quickly becomes unmanageable, but Sprout's Smart Inbox makes handling that chaos feel effortless." — Tolk Persons, Director of Analytics, SKP Creative

**Key insight for Emplifi:** Agency use case is an underserved segment for Emplifi. Cortex could address this with multi-brand/multi-account workflows.

## 6. Paychex — Strengthen Social Responses with AI Assist

**Industry:** HR & Payroll services (800,000 business clients)  
**Challenge:** Organic social team operated in reactive, publishing-only mode; Care team managing social responses in separate platform with legacy systems and spreadsheets; care reps not trained in social media tone

**Sprout AI features used:**
- **Enhance by AI Assist** for reply drafting and refinement
- Rewriting scripted templates into conversational, brand-aligned responses

**Results:**
- Care agents can now draft social-appropriate responses without social media training
- More personable, human-feeling responses at scale
- Reduced time spent on per-response editing

**Quote:**
> "We're not trained on social media. Our agents are thinking about it with a service mindset. Using Sprout's AI functionality to help rewrite responses makes them more personable." — Michelle Latoy, 24/7 Enterprise Manager, Paychex

**Key insight for Emplifi:** Care teams in enterprise brands are often composed of customer service professionals without social media expertise. AI-assisted response drafting closes this skill gap. Relevant for the "WhatsApp Response Templates" and "Smart Escalation Routing" Cortex flows.
